This website is about T.L cosmetics

T.L cosmetics is a youth-driven makeup brand. Its dedicated to providing affordable makeup products.

As a 19-year-old founder, the vision is to create a brand that blends clean ingredients, creative expression, and inclusivity. The initial product line will include lip glosses, tinted balms, lightweight foundations/skin tints, and simple eye products
